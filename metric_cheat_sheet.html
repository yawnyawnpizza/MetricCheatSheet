Metric Cheat Sheet

Welcome to Canada — here's what the numbers mean
🍁
🌡️ Temperature
Cold day 14 °F → −10 °C
Freezing 32 °F → 0 °C
Cool 50 °F → 10 °C
Comfortable 68 °F → 20 °C
Warm 77 °F → 25 °C
Hot 95 °F → 35 °C
Body temp 98.6 °F → 37 °C
Boiling 212 °F → 100 °C
°F → °C: (°F − 32) × 5 ÷ 9  |  Quick trick: subtract 30, divide by 2
📏 Distance
1 inch 2.54 cm
1 foot 30.5 cm
1 yard 0.91 m
1 km 0.62 miles
1 mile 1.6 km
5 miles 8 km
miles → km: × 1.6  |  km → miles: × 0.6
🚗 Speed
30 mph 48 km/h
50 mph 80 km/h
60 mph 100 km/h
75 mph 120 km/h
100 km/h 62 mph
mph → km/h: × 1.6
⚖️ Weight
1 oz 28 g
1 lb 454 g
2.2 lbs 1 kg
100 lbs 45 kg
150 lbs 68 kg
200 lbs 91 kg
lbs → kg: ÷ 2.2  |  kg → lbs: × 2.2
🧴 Volume
1 fl oz 30 mL
1 pint 473 mL
1 quart 0.95 L
1 L ~0.26 gal
1 gallon 3.78 L
Gas tank (avg) ~50–70 L
gallons → litres: × 3.78
